Localize

Welcome to the Cappuccino Community Preview of Localize.

This application is built using the Cappuccino framework. This software was created for The OmniGroup by a team of Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology students for their Senior Project. You can contact the team via the project GMail address.

DISCLAIMER: This software is being released as a development release. We hope that the user experience is enjoyable but we cannot make any promises. This means that any data that you submit to our server may and most likely will be lost to you at a future date. In addition, the team recommends that you use a 'fake' email address as your username (e.g. derek@test.com). This is to reduce the likelihood of your email being intercepted by other parties (we have an open API right now).

The team would like to thank everyone that participates and encourage them to give us oodles of feedback!
